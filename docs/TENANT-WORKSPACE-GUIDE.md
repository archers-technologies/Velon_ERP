# Velon-ERP — Guide for business owners (your workspace)

Simple language. Short read.

---

## What is Velon-ERP?

It is one online system where you can see sales, stock, bills, customers, money coming in and going out, and reports — instead of many spreadsheets and separate tools.

---

## Two different “sides” of the website

1. **Public pages** (home, features, pricing, contact)  
   For anyone browsing. You do not run your business from there. You learn about the product and can ask for a demo or sign in.

2. **Your workspace** (after sign in)  
   This is **your** command center: dashboard, inventory, billing, customers, and more. Only your team should use this part.

---

## Booking a demo

Open **Book demo** (`/demo`). You fill in your details and business type so the sales team can show you the right workflow. The form is a starting point for a live walkthrough — not the same as your daily workspace.

---

## How to try the demo login

Go to **Sign in** (`/login`).

- Use the tenant demo password on the login page (**demo123**) with any work email (not the Super Admin address).
- Use the **super admin** email only if you are testing the platform console; for normal business use, use **any other email** with the demo password to open the **workspace**.

After login you land on your **dashboard** — a summary of “what is happening today.”

---

## What “workspace” means for you

**Workspace** = the place where you run the business in Velon. Think of it as your office inside the app.

The left menu is grouped into three ideas:

1. **Day-to-day work** — Dashboard, Inventory, Billing & POS, Customers, Suppliers.
2. **Money and records** — Accounting, Sales CRM, Reports, Documents.
3. **Helpful tools** — AI Copilot, Automations, Alerts, Branches, Settings.

You move between sections the same way you open folders: one click, one area of the business.

---

## Settings and what you see on screen

In the demo you can change things like **light or dark theme** and how **money** is shown (currency). **Branches** is where multi-location businesses keep stores or offices in one account.

Your real company settings (tax, users, permissions) will grow here as the product is rolled out.

---

## Important note about the demo

The trial screens use **sample data** so you can click around safely. Nothing you see is a promise of legal or tax advice — always confirm with your accountant for real books.
