# STRICT PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

# AI-Ready Universal ERP SaaS Platform

## Product Vision

Build a premium multi-tenant cloud + enterprise deployable Business Operating System that serves universal businesses worldwide. The platform should combine Inventory, Billing, Sales, Accounting, Workforce, Customer Management, Reporting, Subscription Control, and AI-assisted business insights inside one clean modern SaaS ecosystem.

This is not a traditional ERP. This product must feel like a modern Business Command Center with role-based dashboards, minimal smart UI, deep expandable modules, and cross-device usability.
